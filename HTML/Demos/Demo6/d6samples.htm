Tips from Online Scrapbooks

Paste cut-out letters and words in your scrapbook to create a 3-D effect. Online Scrapbooks sells professionally designed cut-out letters, words, and phrases for all occasions.
Clippings, flyers, programs, and other memorabilia are' valuabe sources of information that can enhance your scrapbook pages. Make sure that any material is copied to acid-free paper. Newspaper clippings are especially susceptible to deterioration.
Photographic cut-outs and textured backgrounds can add visual interest to your pages. See the online store for our wide variety of textured and embossed papers.
