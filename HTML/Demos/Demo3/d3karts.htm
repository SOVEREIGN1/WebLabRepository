Get behind the wheel on one of Arcadium's three 1/2 mile paved asphalt tracks. Up to 60 karts can be racing at any one time. Tracks are lit so that you can ride until 10:45 pm each night.

Our go-karts exceed the industry safety standards. Each kart is equipped with roll bars, seatbelts, and webbing for extra protection. Riders must be at least 54 inches in height and 9 years or older to drive alone.

Arcadium supports the following ride options:

  • 7 minutes: $7.00
  • 20 minutes: $12.00
  • 30 minutes: $18.00

Note that Arcadium gold and platinum passes can not be used for go-kart rides.
